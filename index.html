Our Treatment Programs

A full continuum of outpatient care tailored to your stage of recovery.

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Seven days per week, five hours daily of intensive clinical programming. Ideal for individuals transitioning from detox or residential treatment.

  • Individual & group counseling daily
  • Medical and psychiatric care
  • Case management & ancillary services
  • Monday through Sunday scheduling

Learn more →

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Six days per week, three hours daily. Designed for individuals stepping down from PHP who require continued structure and clinical support.

  • Begins at 6 days/week, steps down to 3–5
  • Individualized scheduling
  • All core clinical services included
  • Building independence in recovery

Learn more →

Outpatient Program (OP)

Two days per week of continued care as individuals progress to a self-directed recovery program with ongoing professional support.

  • Individual counseling (1 hour/week)
  • Group therapy session weekly
  • Ongoing psychiatric care
  • Case management & aftercare planning

Why Choose Campus Recovery Center?

Located in the heart of Lake Worth Beach, Florida, Campus Recovery Center (also known as PAX Campus) offers a full continuum of outpatient care for individuals facing substance use disorders and mental health conditions. Our Joint Commission accredited facility combines clinical excellence with a supportive community environment designed to foster lasting recovery.
