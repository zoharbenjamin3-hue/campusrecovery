Why Choose Campus Recovery Center?

Located in the heart of Lake Worth Beach, Florida, Campus Recovery Center (also known as PAX Campus) offers a full continuum of outpatient care for individuals facing substance use disorders and mental health conditions. Our Joint Commission accredited facility combines clinical excellence with a supportive community environment designed to foster lasting recovery.

Our Treatment Programs

Partial Hospitalization (PHP)

Seven days per week, five hours daily of intensive clinical programming. Ideal for individuals transitioning from detox or residential treatment who need structured, comprehensive care while reintegrating into daily life.

Learn more →

Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

Six days per week, three hours daily. Designed for individuals stepping down from PHP who require continued structure, education, and clinical support as they build independence in their recovery.

Learn more →

Outpatient Program (OP)

Two days per week, including individual counseling and group therapy sessions. Provides continued care as individuals progress to a self-directed recovery program with ongoing professional support.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Joint Commission Accredited — Gold Seal of Approval for meeting rigorous quality and safety standards.
  • Highly Credentialed Staff — Licensed, master's-level therapists and certified addiction professionals.
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment — Vivitrol, Naltrexone, and Suboxone available when medically appropriate.
  • Individualized Care Plans — Every client receives a customized treatment plan with a dedicated primary therapist.
  • Full Continuum of Care — PHP, IOP, and OP with seamless transitions between levels.
  • Environment of Care — A collaborative, learning-based community that builds healthy habits and social support.
