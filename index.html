Levels of Care

What Are the Levels of Addiction Treatment?

Not sure where to start? We offer expert guidance to place you at the right level of care before beginning your recovery journey.

01
🏥

Medical Detox

A 3–10 day medically supervised program to safely manage withdrawal symptoms before entering ongoing treatment.

Learn more →
02
🏠

Residential Treatment

Intensive 24/7 residential care that provides comprehensive therapy in a structured, live-in environment.

Learn more →
03
🗂️

Partial Hospitalization (PHP)

Our most intensive outpatient level — 5 hours/day, 7 days/week of structured clinical programming.

Learn more →
04
📅

Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

3 hours/day, 6 days/week — continued clinical support while you rebuild independence in daily life.

Learn more →
05
🌱

Outpatient Program (OP)

Flexible, ongoing care — 2 days/week — as you transition to a self-directed recovery with continued support.

Learn more →
06
🤝

Sober Living

A structured, supportive residence where clients live independently alongside sober peers and house managers.

Learn more →

Treatment Approach

Our Treatment Modalities

Our programs are backed by the latest science in addiction treatment and administered by expert clinicians.

🧠

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Highly effective therapy that helps reframe thought patterns, address underlying causes of addiction, and build coping skills.

⚖️

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Teaches emotional regulation, distress tolerance, and mindfulness to improve relationships and reduce impulsive behavior.

🩺

Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Addresses the common root cause of substance use — mental health — by treating both simultaneously for lasting recovery.

💊

Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)

Prescribed medication that reduces cravings and withdrawal symptoms, making recovery safer and more sustainable.

🌿

Holistic Therapy

Comprehensive care addressing physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being alongside clinical treatment.

👥

Group & Family Therapy

Community-based healing and 12-step facilitation, plus family involvement to rebuild trust and healthy relationships.
