Home › News & Updates › Uncategorized

These articles from the Campus Recovery Center editorial team cover a range of topics related to addiction treatment, regulation, and recovery in South Florida. Written and reviewed by our clinical staff, each article provides in-depth analysis and practical information for individuals, families, and professionals.

  • August 22, 2024

    7 Ways Delray Beach Fought Opioid Addiction

    How a South Florida community coordinated city officials, treatment providers, law enforcement, and residents to combat the opioid crisis and achieve a significant reduction in overdoses. Covers seven specific initiatives including the Sober Homes Task Force, naloxone distribution, and community-based recovery support.

    Author: Daniel Touloumis

  • August 22, 2024

    Is It Safe To Go To A Drug Rehab In Florida?

    Addressing safety concerns about Florida rehab facilities by examining landmark legislation, DCF and AHCA oversight, and the state's transformation into a regulatory model for addiction treatment. Includes a practical checklist for evaluating facilities.

    Author: Daniel Touloumis • Medical Reviewer: Sahil Talwar, PA

  • August 21, 2024

    Can The Addiction Industry Be Taught Regulations From The Kidney Industry?

    A detailed comparison of regulatory frameworks between the addiction treatment industry and the dialysis/kidney care industry. Explores quality monitoring, outcome-based metrics, standardized oversight, and how the addiction field could adopt accountability structures that have improved outcomes in nephrology.

    Author: Freddy Moldt • Medical Reviewer: Sahil Talwar, PA
