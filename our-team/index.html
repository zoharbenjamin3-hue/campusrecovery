Our Team

Author: Daniel Touloumis
Editor: Devin Scheller, Director of Operations
Published: August 1, 2024
Last Edited: March 29, 2026

Campus Recovery Center is home to one of the highest credentialed outpatient clinical teams in the region. Our leadership and clinical staff bring decades of combined experience in mental health, addiction treatment, trauma therapy, and co-occurring disorders.

Leadership

Frank Touloumis

Executive Director

Provides strategic leadership and oversight for all Campus Recovery Center operations, ensuring the highest standards of patient care and organizational excellence.

Devin Scheller

Director of Operations

Oversees daily facility operations and clinical management. Previously served as Clinical Manager, bringing deep institutional knowledge and operational expertise.

Vanessa Mueller

Program Director

Over 15 years of experience in substance abuse and mental health. Oversees daily operations across PHP, IOP, and OP levels of care. Certified in Crisis Intervention, Trauma-Informed Care, Ethics and Confidentiality, and Overdose Prevention.
