Hours of Operation

Campus Recovery Center is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Our admissions team, clinical staff, and support services are available around the clock to assist you.

  • Admissions & intake: Available 24/7 by phone
  • Clinical programming: Monday – Friday, with weekend support available
  • Insurance verification: Same-day verification during business hours; next-day for after-hours inquiries
  • Facility tours: By appointment, Monday – Friday

What to Expect When You Call

We understand that reaching out for help takes courage. When you call Campus Recovery Center, here's what will happen:

  1. Compassionate conversation — You'll speak with a trained admissions counselor who will listen to your situation without judgment. Whether you're calling for yourself, a family member, or a friend, we're here to help.
  2. Brief assessment — We'll ask a few questions about the substance use or mental health challenges you're facing to determine the most appropriate level of care (PHP, IOP, or Outpatient).
  3. Insurance verification — We'll verify your insurance benefits in real time or schedule a callback with coverage details. We accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, United Healthcare, Humana, Tricare, and more.
  4. Next steps — If Campus Recovery Center is the right fit, we'll walk you through the admissions process and schedule your clinical assessment. Many clients begin treatment within 24–48 hours of their first call.

Every call is 100% confidential. There is no obligation, and we will never pressure you into a decision.

Driving Directions

Campus Recovery Center is located at 19 S Dixie Hwy, Lake Worth Beach, FL 33460, on the east side of Dixie Highway (US-1) in downtown Lake Worth Beach. We are easily accessible from all of Palm Beach County.

  • From I-95: Take exit 63 (6th Avenue S / Lake Worth Beach). Head east on 6th Avenue S, then turn right onto S Dixie Hwy (US-1). The facility is on the right.
  • From Delray Beach: Head north on US-1 (S Federal Hwy / S Dixie Hwy) for approximately 7 miles. The facility will be on the right in downtown Lake Worth Beach.
  • From West Palm Beach: Head south on US-1 (S Dixie Hwy) for approximately 6 miles. The facility will be on the left in downtown Lake Worth Beach.
  • From Boynton Beach: Head north on US-1 (N Federal Hwy) for approximately 3 miles into Lake Worth Beach. The facility will be on the right.

Free parking is available on-site and on surrounding streets.

For Referring Providers

If you are a physician, therapist, hospital discharge planner, or treatment facility looking to connect your patients with outpatient care, please contact our admissions team directly at (877) 995-0102.

We work closely with referring providers to ensure seamless transitions of care. Our clinical team can coordinate directly with your office on treatment plans, medication management, and discharge planning. We accept referrals for:

  • Step-down from residential or inpatient treatment
  • Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP) — structured day programming
  • Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) — flexible evening options available
  • Standard Outpatient — ongoing therapy and medication management
  • Dual diagnosis — co-occurring substance use and mental health conditions

To send a referral, email info@campusrecoverycenter.com or call our admissions line. We aim to complete intake within 24–48 hours of referral.
