Ambetter Insurance Coverage for Addiction Treatment

Author: Daniel Touloumis
Editor: Devin Scheller, Director of Operations
Medical Reviewer: Sahil Talwar, PA
Published: August 1, 2024
Last Edited: March 29, 2026

Campus Recovery Center accepts Ambetter insurance for addiction and mental health treatment at our Lake Worth Beach, Florida facility. As an Affordable Care Act marketplace plan, Ambetter is required to cover behavioral health services as an essential health benefit. Our admissions team handles the entire verification process so you can focus on getting well.

About Ambetter Insurance

Ambetter is a line of health insurance products offered through the Health Insurance Marketplace (healthcare.gov) by Centene Corporation. In Florida, Ambetter plans are administered by Sunshine Health and are available to individuals and families who purchase coverage during open enrollment or qualifying life events. Ambetter offers Bronze, Silver, and Gold tier plans across many Florida counties, including Palm Beach County.

Because Ambetter is sold on the ACA marketplace, every plan must comply with federal requirements for essential health benefits—including substance use disorder and mental health treatment. This means Ambetter members have a legal right to behavioral health coverage under their plan.

Behavioral Health Benefits Under Ambetter

The Affordable Care Act requires all marketplace plans, including Ambetter, to cover mental health and substance use disorder services as one of ten essential health benefit categories. Combined with the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act (MHPAEA), Ambetter must provide behavioral health benefits at levels comparable to medical and surgical coverage.

Ambetter behavioral health benefits generally include:

  • Substance use disorder assessment and diagnosis
  • Individual, group, and family counseling
  • Psychiatric evaluation and medication management
  • Crisis intervention services
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT) for opioid and alcohol use disorders
  • Structured outpatient treatment programs

Levels of Care Covered by Ambetter

Campus Recovery Center provides several levels of outpatient addiction and mental health treatment. Ambetter plans typically cover the following programs when deemed medically necessary:

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Our PHP provides intensive, structured treatment five to seven days per week. Clients attend programming for approximately six hours per day and return home or to a sober living environment in the evening. Ambetter covers PHP when clinical criteria demonstrate the need for a high level of outpatient care, such as following residential treatment or during acute stabilization.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

IOP offers three to five days of programming per week for approximately three hours per session. This level of care allows clients to maintain work, school, or family obligations while receiving evidence-based treatment. Ambetter plans cover IOP as a step-down from PHP or as an entry point for individuals who do not require full-day programming.

Outpatient Program (OP)

Standard outpatient treatment includes individual therapy, group counseling, and psychiatric appointments on a weekly or biweekly basis. Ambetter covers outpatient behavioral health visits as part of its essential health benefits, subject to your plan's copay and coinsurance structure.

Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)

MAT combines FDA-approved medications—such as buprenorphine (Suboxone), naltrexone (Vivitrol), or acamprosate—with counseling and behavioral therapies. Ambetter is required to cover MAT medications and the associated clinical services under ACA essential health benefit mandates.

Ambetter Plans in Florida

Ambetter offers several plan tiers in Florida through the Health Insurance Marketplace. Each tier determines your monthly premium, deductible, copays, and coinsurance rates:

  • Ambetter Bronze – Lower monthly premiums with higher out-of-pocket costs. Behavioral health services are covered after meeting the annual deductible.
  • Ambetter Silver – Moderate premiums and deductibles. Silver plans may qualify for cost-sharing reductions that lower copays and deductibles for behavioral health visits.
  • Ambetter Gold – Higher premiums with lower deductibles and copays. Gold plans typically provide the most affordable per-visit costs for ongoing treatment.

Many Ambetter members in Florida qualify for premium tax credits and cost-sharing reductions that significantly lower treatment costs. If you purchased your Ambetter plan through healthcare.gov, you may already be receiving these subsidies.

ACA Essential Health Benefits and Addiction Treatment

Under the Affordable Care Act, substance use disorder and mental health treatment are classified as essential health benefits. This federal mandate applies to all Ambetter plans and ensures that:

  • Pre-existing conditions, including prior substance use disorders, cannot be used to deny coverage
  • Annual and lifetime dollar limits on behavioral health benefits are prohibited
  • Behavioral health cost-sharing (copays, deductibles, coinsurance) cannot be more restrictive than medical or surgical cost-sharing
  • Prior authorization requirements for behavioral health must be comparable to those for medical services
  • Coverage for MAT medications must be included in the plan formulary

What to Expect with Ambetter Coverage

Ambetter uses a managed care model, which means your plan may require prior authorization before starting certain levels of care such as PHP or IOP. Campus Recovery Center handles the prior authorization process on your behalf, working directly with Ambetter's utilization management team to obtain approval.

Key details to keep in mind:

  • Network Status: Confirm whether Campus Recovery Center is in-network with your specific Ambetter plan. In-network providers offer the lowest out-of-pocket costs.
  • Deductible: Depending on your plan tier, you may need to meet your annual deductible before Ambetter covers treatment costs.
  • Copays and Coinsurance: After meeting your deductible, you will typically pay a copay per visit or a coinsurance percentage of the allowed amount.
  • Out-of-Pocket Maximum: All ACA plans have an annual out-of-pocket maximum. Once you reach this limit, Ambetter covers 100% of covered services for the remainder of the plan year.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Ambetter cover addiction treatment?

Yes. Ambetter is an ACA marketplace plan, so it must cover substance use disorder treatment as an essential health benefit under federal law. This includes PHP, IOP, outpatient counseling, psychiatric services, and Medication-Assisted Treatment. Specific cost-sharing depends on your plan tier.

What levels of care does Ambetter cover at Campus Recovery Center?

Ambetter plans typically cover Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP), Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP), standard outpatient therapy, psychiatric services, and Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT). Coverage is subject to medical necessity criteria and may require prior authorization.

How do I verify my Ambetter benefits for rehab?

Call Campus Recovery Center at (877) 995-0102. Our admissions team contacts Ambetter directly, verifies your behavioral health benefits, and explains your copay, deductible, and out-of-pocket costs before treatment begins. The verification process is free and confidential.

Is Ambetter available in Florida?

Yes. Ambetter plans are offered in Florida through the Health Insurance Marketplace and administered by Sunshine Health. Coverage is available in many Florida counties, including Palm Beach County where Campus Recovery Center is located in Lake Worth Beach.
