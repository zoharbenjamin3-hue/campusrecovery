Campus Recovery Center accepts TRICARE insurance for addiction and mental health treatment at our Lake Worth Beach, Florida facility. TRICARE is the health care program for uniformed service members, retirees, and their families, providing comprehensive behavioral health benefits including substance use disorder and mental health treatment across multiple levels of outpatient care.

TRICARE Behavioral Health Coverage

TRICARE provides behavioral health benefits that cover substance use disorder treatment and mental health services for eligible beneficiaries. TRICARE recognizes that service members, veterans, and their families may face unique behavioral health challenges, and the program provides coverage for evidence-based treatment at authorized facilities. TRICARE behavioral health benefits include coverage for addiction treatment, dual diagnosis care, and psychiatric services.

As a Department of Defense health care program, TRICARE covers behavioral health treatment without the same parity law framework as commercial insurance, but the program provides comprehensive substance use disorder and mental health benefits to all eligible beneficiaries.

TRICARE Plans Accepted

Campus Recovery Center works with TRICARE beneficiaries across multiple plan types, including:

  • TRICARE Prime – Managed care option requiring enrollment and a primary care manager referral for behavioral health services
  • TRICARE Select – Self-managed preferred provider option that allows self-referral to network behavioral health providers
  • TRICARE For Life – Medicare wraparound coverage for military retirees and their families who are Medicare-eligible
  • TRICARE Reserve Select – Coverage for qualified members of the National Guard and Reserve
  • TRICARE Young Adult – Coverage for qualified adult children of eligible uniformed service members up to age 26

Coverage details, cost-shares, and copays vary by plan and beneficiary category (active duty, retiree, dependent). Our admissions team verifies your specific TRICARE benefits before treatment begins.

Treatment Programs Covered by TRICARE

TRICARE behavioral health benefits typically cover the following levels of care at Campus Recovery Center:

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Structured programming five to seven days per week for individuals who need intensive clinical support. PHP includes individual therapy, group counseling, psychiatric evaluation, and medication management. TRICARE covers PHP when medical necessity criteria are met and prior authorization is obtained.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Flexible programming three to five days per week, allowing clients to maintain responsibilities while receiving treatment. IOP includes evidence-based therapies, relapse prevention, and clinical support. TRICARE covers IOP for both substance use and mental health conditions.

Outpatient Treatment (OP)

Individual counseling, group therapy, and psychiatric services on a less intensive schedule. Outpatient treatment serves as a step-down from IOP or as standalone care. TRICARE covers outpatient behavioral health visits for eligible beneficiaries.

Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)

FDA-approved medications combined with counseling and behavioral therapies for opioid and alcohol use disorders. TRICARE covers MAT medications including buprenorphine, naltrexone, and other prescribed treatments when part of a comprehensive treatment plan.

Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Integrated care for individuals with both a substance use disorder and a co-occurring mental health condition such as depression, anxiety, PTSD, or bipolar disorder. TRICARE behavioral health benefits cover dual diagnosis treatment, which is particularly important for service members and veterans who may experience combat-related trauma alongside substance use.

How to Verify Your TRICARE Benefits

  1. Call Our Admissions Team

    Contact Campus Recovery Center at (877) 995-0102. Have your TRICARE beneficiary information ready, including your Department of Defense (DoD) ID number and TRICARE plan type. Our admissions team is available to help.

  2. We Verify Your TRICARE Benefits

    Our insurance verification specialists confirm your TRICARE behavioral health benefits. We determine your coverage for PHP, IOP, outpatient services, and MAT, and identify any referral or prior authorization requirements based on your plan type.

  3. Review Your Benefits

    We explain your TRICARE coverage in plain language—what treatment is covered, your cost-share or copay amounts, and any session or day limits that apply to your plan and beneficiary category.

  4. Begin Treatment

    Once your TRICARE benefits are verified and any required authorizations are in place, our team coordinates the clinical intake process so you can begin treatment as quickly as possible.

TRICARE Referral and Authorization Requirements

TRICARE referral and prior authorization requirements depend on your plan type. TRICARE Prime beneficiaries generally need a referral from their primary care manager (PCM) before receiving behavioral health services. TRICARE Select beneficiaries can typically self-refer to network behavioral health providers without a referral. Certain levels of care, such as partial hospitalization, may require prior authorization regardless of your plan type.

Our admissions team handles the referral and authorization process on your behalf, coordinating with your PCM and TRICARE regional contractor to ensure all requirements are met before treatment begins.

What Information Do I Need to Verify TRICARE Coverage?

When you call to verify your TRICARE benefits, have the following information available:

  • Department of Defense (DoD) ID number or Defense Enrollment Eligibility Reporting System (DEERS) information
  • TRICARE plan type (Prime, Select, For Life, Reserve Select, or Young Adult)
  • Sponsor name, rank, and branch of service
  • Beneficiary name and date of birth
  • TRICARE regional contractor information (if known)

Frequently Asked Questions

Does TRICARE cover addiction treatment?

Yes. TRICARE covers substance use disorder treatment including partial hospitalization, intensive outpatient, outpatient counseling, and medication-assisted treatment. TRICARE behavioral health benefits are available to active duty service members, retirees, and eligible dependents at Campus Recovery Center.

Does TRICARE cover mental health treatment at Campus Recovery Center?

Yes. Campus Recovery Center accepts TRICARE for mental health treatment including dual diagnosis care, psychiatric evaluation, medication management, and individual and group therapy across all outpatient levels of care.

How do I verify my TRICARE benefits for rehab?

Call Campus Recovery Center at (877) 995-0102. Our admissions team verifies your TRICARE behavioral health benefits, confirms coverage for specific treatment programs, and explains any cost-sharing requirements including copays and cost-shares.

Do I need a referral to use TRICARE for addiction treatment?

Referral requirements depend on your TRICARE plan. TRICARE Prime typically requires a referral from your primary care manager, while TRICARE Select allows you to self-refer to network behavioral health providers. Our admissions team can help determine your referral requirements and coordinate the process.
