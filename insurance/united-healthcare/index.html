United Healthcare Coverage for Addiction Treatment

Campus Recovery Center accepts United Healthcare (UHC) insurance for addiction and mental health treatment at our Lake Worth Beach, Florida facility.

Call (877) 995-0102

Campus Recovery Center accepts United Healthcare (UHC) insurance for addiction and mental health treatment at our Lake Worth Beach, Florida facility. As one of the largest health insurers in the United States, UHC provides behavioral health benefits through Optum, covering a range of substance use disorder and mental health services. Our admissions team handles the entire verification process so you can focus on recovery.

UHC Behavioral Health Benefits Through Optum

United Healthcare manages its behavioral health and substance use disorder benefits through Optum, a subsidiary that oversees mental health and addiction treatment authorizations. When you use your UHC plan at Campus Recovery Center, Optum reviews and authorizes your care based on medical necessity criteria. Our clinical team works directly with Optum throughout your treatment to ensure continued authorization and uninterrupted care.

United Healthcare Plans We Accept

Campus Recovery Center works with most United Healthcare plan types, including:

  • UHC Employer-Sponsored Plans — Group coverage provided through your employer, including PPO, HMO, EPO, and POS plans
  • UHC Individual & Family Plans — ACA marketplace plans purchased through HealthCare.gov or directly from UHC
  • UHC Medicare Advantage — Medicare plans administered by United Healthcare for adults 65 and older
  • UHC Community Plan (Medicaid) — Medicaid managed care plans in participating states
  • UHC Student Resources — University and college student health plans
  • UHC Global — International and expatriate coverage

Coverage levels, copays, and deductibles vary by plan. Call (877) 995-0102 to verify your specific benefits.

Treatment Programs Covered by UHC

United Healthcare behavioral health benefits typically cover the following levels of care at Campus Recovery Center:

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

PHP provides structured, full-day treatment five to seven days per week. UHC covers PHP for substance use disorders and co-occurring mental health conditions when medically necessary. Optum authorizes PHP treatment in increments, and our team manages the reauthorization process throughout your stay.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

IOP offers structured group and individual therapy sessions three to five days per week, typically three or more hours per day. UHC plans cover IOP for addiction and mental health treatment, often as a step-down from PHP or as an entry point for individuals who do not require full-day programming.

Outpatient Services (OP)

Standard outpatient therapy—including individual counseling, group therapy, and psychiatric appointments—is covered under most UHC plans. Outpatient services support long-term recovery after completing PHP or IOP.

Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)

UHC covers medications used to treat opioid and alcohol use disorders, including buprenorphine (Suboxone), naltrexone (Vivitrol), and other FDA-approved medications. MAT is integrated into all levels of care at Campus Recovery Center and is covered as part of your behavioral health benefits.

Psychiatric and Medical Services

Psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and medical assessments performed during treatment are covered under UHC behavioral health and medical benefits. Campus Recovery Center coordinates both behavioral health and medical billing to simplify the process for patients.

How UHC Prior Authorization Works

Most United Healthcare plans require prior authorization before beginning addiction treatment. Here is how the process works at Campus Recovery Center:

  1. Call Our Admissions Team

    Contact us at (877) 995-0102. Provide your UHC member ID, group number, and basic information. Our team is available 24/7.

  2. We Verify Benefits With Optum

    Our team contacts Optum directly to verify your behavioral health benefits, confirm in-network or out-of-network status, and determine your copay, deductible, coinsurance, and out-of-pocket maximum.

  3. Prior Authorization Is Submitted

    We submit the prior authorization request to Optum with clinical documentation supporting medical necessity. Most authorizations are processed within 24 to 48 hours.

  4. Treatment Begins

    Once authorization is confirmed, you can begin treatment. Our team explains your coverage, expected costs, and what to expect before your first day.

Your Rights Under Federal Law

Under the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act (MHPAEA) and the Affordable Care Act (ACA), United Healthcare is required to cover substance use disorder and mental health treatment at levels comparable to medical and surgical care. This means your UHC plan cannot impose stricter limits on addiction treatment than it applies to physical health conditions, including visit limits, prior authorization requirements, and cost-sharing.

If your UHC plan denies coverage for addiction treatment, you have the right to appeal the decision. Campus Recovery Center assists with the appeals process and can provide clinical documentation to support your case.

What to Have Ready When You Call

To verify your UHC benefits quickly, have the following information available:

  • Your UHC member ID number (found on your insurance card)
  • Group number (if applicable)
  • Subscriber name and date of birth
  • Type of treatment you are seeking (addiction, mental health, or both)

Frequently Asked Questions

Does United Healthcare cover addiction treatment?

Yes. United Healthcare covers substance use disorder and mental health treatment under its behavioral health benefits, managed through Optum. Coverage typically includes PHP, IOP, outpatient therapy, MAT, and psychiatric services. Specific benefits depend on your plan type and network status.

Does UHC require prior authorization for rehab?

Most UHC plans require prior authorization for behavioral health treatment, particularly for PHP and IOP programs. Campus Recovery Center handles the prior authorization process on your behalf, working directly with Optum to secure approval before treatment begins.

What UHC plans does Campus Recovery Center accept?

We accept most United Healthcare plan types including employer-sponsored plans, individual and family plans, Medicare Advantage, Community Plan (Medicaid), Student Resources plans, and UHC Global coverage. Call (877) 995-0102 to verify your specific plan.

How do I verify my United Healthcare benefits for rehab?

Call Campus Recovery Center at (877) 995-0102. Our admissions team contacts Optum directly to verify your behavioral health benefits, confirm your coverage levels, and explain your copay, deductible, and out-of-pocket costs before treatment begins.
