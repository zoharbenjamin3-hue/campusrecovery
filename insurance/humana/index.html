Humana Insurance Coverage for Addiction & Mental Health Treatment

Campus Recovery Center accepts Humana insurance for addiction and mental health treatment at our Lake Worth Beach, Florida facility.

Campus Recovery Center accepts Humana insurance for addiction and mental health treatment at our Lake Worth Beach, Florida facility. Humana is one of the largest health insurance providers in the United States, serving millions of members through employer-sponsored plans, individual marketplace plans, Medicare Advantage plans, and Medicaid managed care. Humana provides behavioral health coverage for substance use disorder and mental health services across multiple levels of outpatient care.

Humana Behavioral Health Coverage

Humana plans include behavioral health benefits for substance use disorder and mental health treatment. Under the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act (MHPAEA) and the Affordable Care Act (ACA), Humana is required to cover behavioral health treatment at levels comparable to medical and surgical care. This means your Humana plan likely covers treatment at Campus Recovery Center.

Humana manages behavioral health services through its integrated behavioral health program, which coordinates care for members seeking substance use disorder treatment, mental health counseling, and psychiatric services.

Humana Plans Accepted

Campus Recovery Center works with most Humana plan types, including:

  • Humana PPO – Preferred Provider Organization plans with in-network and out-of-network behavioral health benefits
  • Humana HMO – Health Maintenance Organization plans with behavioral health coverage through network providers
  • Humana Gold Plus HMO – Medicare Advantage plans with integrated behavioral health benefits
  • Humana Medicare Advantage – Plans for individuals 65 and older that include behavioral health coverage
  • Humana Employer-Sponsored Plans – Group health plans offered through employers with behavioral health coverage
  • Humana Marketplace Plans – Individual and family plans purchased through the Health Insurance Marketplace with essential health benefits including behavioral health

Coverage details, copays, deductibles, and out-of-pocket maximums vary by plan. Our admissions team verifies your specific Humana policy before treatment begins.

Treatment Programs Covered by Humana

Humana behavioral health benefits typically cover the following levels of care at Campus Recovery Center:

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Structured programming five to seven days per week for individuals who need intensive clinical support. PHP includes individual therapy, group counseling, psychiatric evaluation, and medication management. Humana typically covers PHP when medical necessity criteria are met.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Flexible programming three to five days per week, allowing clients to maintain work, school, or family responsibilities while receiving treatment. IOP includes evidence-based therapies, relapse prevention, and clinical support. Humana plans commonly cover IOP for both substance use and mental health conditions.

Outpatient Treatment (OP)

Individual counseling, group therapy, and psychiatric services on a less intensive schedule. Outpatient treatment serves as a step-down from IOP or as standalone care. Most Humana plans cover outpatient behavioral health visits.

Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT)

FDA-approved medications combined with counseling and behavioral therapies for opioid and alcohol use disorders. Humana covers MAT medications including buprenorphine, naltrexone, and other prescribed treatments when part of a comprehensive treatment plan.

Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Integrated care for individuals with both a substance use disorder and a co-occurring mental health condition such as depression, anxiety, PTSD, or bipolar disorder. Humana behavioral health benefits cover dual diagnosis treatment when clinical criteria are satisfied.

How to Verify Your Humana Insurance

  1. Call Our Admissions Team

    Contact Campus Recovery Center at (877) 995-0102. Have your Humana insurance card ready, including your member ID and group number. Our admissions team is available to help.

  2. We Contact Humana Directly

    Our insurance verification specialists contact Humana to confirm your behavioral health benefits. We determine your coverage for PHP, IOP, outpatient services, and MAT, and identify any prior authorization requirements.

  3. Review Your Benefits

    We explain your Humana coverage in plain language—what treatment is covered, your copay and deductible amounts, your out-of-pocket maximum, and any session or day limits that apply to your plan.

  4. Begin Treatment

    Once your Humana benefits are verified, our team coordinates the clinical intake process so you can begin treatment as quickly as possible.

Humana Behavioral Health Network

Humana manages its behavioral health benefits through an integrated network of mental health and substance use disorder treatment providers. Campus Recovery Center works with Humana to help our clients access their behavioral health benefits. Whether you have in-network or out-of-network coverage, our admissions team can clarify what your Humana plan covers at our facility.

Many Humana PPO plans do not require a referral for outpatient behavioral health services, though some HMO plans may have referral or prior authorization requirements. Our team handles the authorization process on your behalf.

What Information Do I Need to Verify Humana Coverage?

When you call to verify your Humana insurance, have the following information available:

  • Humana member ID number (found on your insurance card)
  • Group number (if applicable)
  • Policyholder name and date of birth
  • Patient name and date of birth (if different from the policyholder)
  • Humana customer service phone number (on the back of your card)

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Humana cover addiction treatment?

Yes. Humana plans are required under federal parity laws to cover substance use disorder treatment at levels comparable to medical and surgical care. This includes partial hospitalization, intensive outpatient, outpatient counseling, and medication-assisted treatment at Campus Recovery Center.

Does Humana cover mental health treatment at Campus Recovery Center?

Yes. Campus Recovery Center accepts Humana for mental health treatment including dual diagnosis care, psychiatric evaluation, medication management, and individual and group therapy across all outpatient levels of care.

How do I verify my Humana insurance benefits for rehab?

Call Campus Recovery Center at (877) 995-0102. Our admissions team contacts Humana directly to verify your behavioral health benefits, confirm coverage for specific treatment programs, and explain your copay, deductible, and out-of-pocket costs.

What Humana plans does Campus Recovery Center accept?

We accept most Humana plans including Humana PPO, Humana HMO, Humana Gold Plus, Humana Medicare Advantage, and Humana employer-sponsored plans. Coverage details vary by plan, so contact our admissions team to verify your specific policy.
