Stay informed with the latest research, news, and insights on addiction treatment, mental health, and recovery from the Campus Recovery Center team. Our clinical staff and leadership contribute original articles exploring industry regulation, community-level responses to the addiction crisis, and practical guidance for anyone considering treatment in Florida.

Each article is written or reviewed by credentialed professionals on our team, including our founder Daniel Touloumis, clinical director Freddy Moldt, and medical reviewer Sahil Talwar, PA.

Recent Articles

  • August 22, 2024

    7 Ways Delray Beach Fought Opioid Addiction

    How a South Florida community coordinated city officials, treatment providers, law enforcement, and residents to combat the opioid crisis and achieve a significant reduction in overdoses. This article examines seven specific initiatives — from the Sober Homes Task Force to naloxone distribution programs — that helped Delray Beach become a national model for community-driven addiction response.

    Author: Daniel Touloumis

  • August 22, 2024

    Is It Safe To Go To A Drug Rehab In Florida?

    Addressing safety concerns about Florida rehab facilities, examining landmark legislation like the Patient Brokering Act and Marchman Act, oversight bodies including DCF and AHCA, and the state's transformation from the "Wild West" of addiction treatment into a regulatory model. Includes a practical checklist for evaluating any Florida facility.

    Author: Daniel Touloumis • Medical Reviewer: Sahil Talwar, PA

  • August 21, 2024

    Can The Addiction Industry Be Taught Regulations From The Kidney Industry?

    A comparison of regulatory frameworks between the addiction treatment industry and the dialysis/kidney industry, exploring quality monitoring, outcome-based metrics, and standardized oversight. The article argues that the addiction treatment industry could adopt many of the same accountability structures that have improved outcomes in nephrology care.

    Author: Freddy Moldt • Medical Reviewer: Sahil Talwar, PA

Topics We Cover

Our editorial team writes about subjects that matter to individuals and families navigating addiction and mental health treatment:

  • Addiction science & research — New findings on substance use disorders, brain science, and evidence-based treatment approaches
  • Treatment industry regulation — How legislation, accreditation, and oversight affect the quality of care you receive
  • Florida-specific resources — State programs, local community efforts, and what makes South Florida a hub for recovery
  • Mental health & dual diagnosis — The intersection of substance use and conditions like depression, anxiety, PTSD, and bipolar disorder
  • Recovery & aftercare — Practical guidance on maintaining sobriety, building support networks, and transitioning back to daily life
  • Family & loved ones — Resources for family members supporting someone through treatment and recovery

About Our Authors
