Careers at Campus Recovery Center

Author: Daniel Touloumis
Editor: Devin Scheller, Director of Operations
Published: August 1, 2024
Last Edited: March 29, 2026

Campus Recovery Center is always looking for compassionate, dedicated professionals who are passionate about helping individuals recover from addiction and mental health conditions. Join our team and make a meaningful difference in people's lives every day.

Why Work With Us

  • Mission-driven work — Help clients transform their lives through evidence-based treatment.
  • Collaborative environment — Work alongside a team of highly credentialed clinical professionals.
  • Joint Commission accredited — Be part of a facility recognized for meeting rigorous quality and safety standards.
  • Professional development — Opportunities for continuing education and career growth in the behavioral health field.
  • South Florida location — Work in the heart of Lake Worth Beach, Florida.

Areas We Hire For

Clinical Staff

Licensed therapists, mental health counselors, certified addiction professionals, and registered interns with experience in substance use disorders, mental health treatment, and co-occurring conditions.

Medical Staff

Physicians, physician assistants, psychiatric nurse practitioners, and registered nurses with experience in behavioral health, addiction medicine, and psychiatric evaluation.

Support Staff

Case managers, behavioral health technicians, outreach coordinators, alumni coordinators, and administrative professionals who share our commitment to client care.

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining our team, please send your resume with contact information to:

  • Email: info@paxcampus.com
  • Phone: (877) 995-0102

We review all applications carefully and will contact qualified candidates for an interview.
