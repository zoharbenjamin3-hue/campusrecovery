Daniel Touloumis

Founder & CEO

Daniel Touloumis leads Campus Recovery Center with a singular mission: to make evidence-based addiction treatment accessible, effective, and grounded in genuine compassion. Under his leadership, Campus Recovery Center has become a trusted provider of outpatient substance use and mental health services in South Florida.

About Daniel

Daniel founded Campus Recovery Center with a vision to provide accessible, evidence-based addiction treatment in South Florida. His commitment to combining clinical excellence with compassionate care drives the center's mission. Recognizing the gaps in the behavioral healthcare system, Daniel set out to build a treatment center that puts clients first -- one where every individual receives personalized, research-backed care in a supportive environment.

With extensive experience in behavioral healthcare leadership, Daniel has developed a deep understanding of the challenges facing those struggling with substance use disorders and co-occurring mental health conditions. His hands-on approach and dedication to continuous improvement ensure that Campus Recovery Center remains at the forefront of addiction treatment best practices.

Leadership & Vision

As Founder and CEO, Daniel sets the strategic direction for Campus Recovery Center. He is committed to maintaining the highest standards of care, which is reflected in the center's Joint Commission accreditation and the caliber of its clinical team. Daniel works closely with licensed therapists, certified addiction professionals, and medical staff to ensure that treatment programs are grounded in the latest evidence and tailored to each client's unique needs.

Under Daniel's leadership, Campus Recovery Center offers a comprehensive continuum of care -- from Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP) and Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP) to Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT) and individual therapy. His vision extends beyond clinical outcomes; he is focused on building a recovery community where clients feel supported long after they complete their program.

Commitment to Recovery

Daniel believes that recovery is possible for everyone. His philosophy centers on treating the whole person -- addressing not only the addiction itself but also the underlying mental health conditions, trauma, and life circumstances that contribute to substance use. This integrated approach has helped countless individuals and families find lasting recovery.

He is also a strong advocate for reducing the stigma surrounding addiction and mental health treatment. Through community outreach, education, and partnerships with local organizations, Daniel works to ensure that more people have access to the help they need.

Education & Credentials

Daniel brings extensive experience in behavioral healthcare leadership to his role at Campus Recovery Center. His background spans program development, operational management, and clinical oversight, giving him a comprehensive perspective on what it takes to deliver effective addiction treatment at scale.
