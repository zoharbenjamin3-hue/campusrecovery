Our 3-Step Admissions Process

>>>>>>> 46499058ef4c70c2e04eedd367c7e947e86bad71

  1. Connect

    Contact our admissions team by phone at (877) 995-0102 or email at info@campusrecoverycenter.com. We'll discuss your needs, answer your questions, and help you explore the treatment options that are right for you or your loved one. Our team is available around the clock.

  2. Verify & Assess

    We verify your insurance coverage quickly and confidentially, then conduct a thorough clinical assessment. This assessment helps our clinical team understand your unique situation and create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs.

  3. Begin Treatment

    Visit our facility at 19 S Dixie Hwy, Lake Worth Beach, FL to complete intake paperwork and begin your recovery program. Your primary therapist and care team will be ready to welcome you and get you started on your individualized treatment plan.

Treatment Programs Available

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Our most intensive outpatient option: 7 days/week, 5 hours/day. Ideal for individuals reintegrating into daily living after detox or residential treatment.

Learn more →

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Structured support with increasing independence: begins at 6 days/week, 3 hours/day, stepping down as recovery strengthens.

Learn more →

Outpatient Program (OP)

Continued care for individuals progressing to self-directed recovery: 2 days/week of individual and group counseling.

Learn more →

Insurance & Payment

Campus Recovery Center accepts most major insurance plans. Our admissions team will verify your coverage, explain your benefits, and prepare all necessary claims on your behalf. Self-pay and financial assistance options are also available.

Don't let cost be a barrier to treatment. Call us to discuss your options—we'll work with you to find a solution.

What to Expect

  • A compassionate, judgment-free intake process
  • A primary therapist assigned for the full duration of your program
  • Comprehensive health assessments and psychiatric evaluation
  • An individualized treatment plan created specifically for your needs
  • Access to all clinical services from day one
  • Case management support for practical needs

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I start treatment?

In many cases, treatment can begin within 24–48 hours of your initial call. Our team works to expedite the admissions and insurance verification process so you can begin recovery as soon as possible.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is required. You, a family member, or another treatment provider can contact our admissions team directly to begin the process.

Can I tour the facility?

Yes. We welcome and encourage facility tours. Contact our team to schedule a visit so you can see our campus and meet our staff before beginning treatment.
