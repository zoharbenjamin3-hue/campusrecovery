Our Treatment Programs Serving Delray Beach

Campus Recovery Center offers three levels of outpatient care, each designed to meet individuals at the appropriate stage of their recovery:

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Our PHP provides five hours of intensive clinical programming seven days per week. This is our highest level of outpatient care and is ideal for individuals transitioning from detox, residential treatment, or those who need significant daily structure. Services include individual and group therapy, psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and comprehensive case management.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

The IOP offers three hours of daily programming, beginning at six days per week and gradually stepping down. This program bridges the gap between PHP and independent recovery, providing continued therapeutic engagement while encouraging greater autonomy in daily life.

Outpatient Program (OP)

Our outpatient program provides two days per week of ongoing clinical support, including individual counseling, group therapy, and psychiatric care. This level is designed for clients who have established a solid foundation in recovery and are transitioning to a more self-directed lifestyle.
