Our Treatment Programs

Campus Recovery Center provides a full continuum of outpatient care designed to meet clients at every stage of their recovery journey. Our Lake Worth Beach addiction treatment programs include:

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Our most intensive level of care, the PHP program offers five hours of clinical programming seven days per week. This program is ideal for individuals stepping down from detox or residential treatment, or those who need a high level of structure and support. Services include individual counseling, group therapy, psychiatric care, medication management, and case management.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

The IOP provides three hours of daily programming, beginning at six days per week and stepping down as clinically appropriate. This program is designed for clients transitioning from PHP who still benefit from regular therapeutic engagement while beginning to build greater independence in their recovery.

Outpatient Program (OP)

Our outpatient program offers two days per week of continued support, including individual counseling, group therapy, and ongoing psychiatric care. This level of care is appropriate for individuals who have made meaningful progress and are ready for a more self-directed approach to their recovery.
