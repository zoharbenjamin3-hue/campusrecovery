Our Treatment Programs Serving Boynton Beach

We provide a complete spectrum of outpatient addiction and mental health treatment, ensuring that each Boynton Beach client receives care matched to their current needs:

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Our PHP delivers five hours of intensive clinical programming seven days per week. This program is suited for individuals who require a high level of structure and daily engagement, whether stepping down from residential care or entering treatment for the first time. PHP includes individual therapy, group counseling, psychiatric services, and holistic case management.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

The IOP consists of three hours of daily programming, initially offered six days per week and adjusting as the client progresses. This level of care allows Boynton Beach residents to begin resuming work, school, or family responsibilities while maintaining consistent therapeutic support.

Outpatient Program (OP)

Our OP provides two sessions per week, including individual counseling and group therapy. This program supports clients who have achieved stability in their recovery and are preparing for independent, long-term sobriety with ongoing professional guidance.
