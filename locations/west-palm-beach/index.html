Addiction Treatment Near West Palm Beach, Florida

As the county seat, it is home to a diverse population that includes many individuals and families affected by substance use disorders and co-occurring mental health conditions. Our facility at 19 S Dixie Hwy offers a full continuum of outpatient care, from intensive PHP programming to step-down IOP and outpatient services. For West Palm Beach residents, this means high-quality drug rehab and mental health treatment is available just a short drive down I-95 or US-1, close enough to maintain your daily responsibilities while committing fully to recovery.

Our Treatment Programs Serving West Palm Beach

Campus Recovery Center provides three structured levels of outpatient addiction treatment, allowing West Palm Beach residents to receive care matched to the severity of their condition and adjusted as they progress:

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

The PHP offers five hours of intensive clinical programming seven days per week. This is our most structured outpatient level and is appropriate for individuals transitioning from inpatient or residential care, as well as those entering treatment for the first time who need daily clinical engagement. PHP services include individual therapy, process groups, psychoeducation, psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and care coordination.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Our IOP provides three hours of daily programming, starting at six days per week and tapering as clinically indicated. West Palm Beach clients in IOP benefit from continued therapeutic structure while gaining the flexibility to return to work, school, or family responsibilities during non-treatment hours.

Outpatient Program (OP)

The OP offers two sessions per week, combining individual counseling with group therapy and ongoing psychiatric care. This program is designed for individuals who have developed a strong foundation in recovery and are preparing for long-term, independent sobriety.

West Palm Beach Community and Recovery Resources

West Palm Beach offers a robust network of recovery resources. The city is home to numerous AA and NA meeting groups, the Palm Beach County Substance Awareness Coalition, and multiple sober living communities. JFK Medical Center's behavioral health unit and the Jerome Golden Center for Behavioral Health provide crisis and inpatient services for those who need stabilization before stepping down to outpatient care at Campus Recovery Center. The county's Southeast Florida Behavioral Health Network also coordinates resources for individuals seeking treatment assistance.
