What Are The Levels of Addiction Treatment?

### 1\. Detox

A 3-10 day program that provides a safe, medically supervised environment to get through any painful or dangerous withdrawal symptoms.

[Drug Detox](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/detox/)

### 2\. Inpatient Treatment

A 20-25 day program that provides intensive clinical therapy to treat substance abuse in a residential setting.

[Inpatient Treatment](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/inpatient-rehab/)

### 3\. Partial Hospitalization   Program (PHP)

Treatment in a supervised setting with more freedom than inpatient care, allowing clients to start applying recovery skills in daily life.

[PHP Treatment](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/php-partial-hospitalization-program/)

### 4\. Intensive Outpatient   Program (IOP)

A short, intensive program that provides support while allowing clients to gradually return to their daily routines.

[IOP Treatment](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/intensive-outpatient/)

### 5\. Outpatient Program (OP)

A less version of IOP, lasting about 1 hour a day, helping clients maintain support while returning to daily life.

[Outpatient Treatment](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/outpatient/)

### 6\. Sober Living

Sober living offers a supportive residence where clients can live independently in a safe, structured environment alongside sober peers.

[Sober Living](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/sober-living/)

### Dual Diagnosis

Dual Diagnosis addresses a common root cause of substance abuse—mental health. Treating both simultaneously is the most effective for sustained recovery.

[Dual Diagnosis Treatment](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/dual-diagnosis/)

### Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

DBT is a cognitive therapy that teaches skills to help regulate emotions, improve relationships, control impulsive behavior, & become more present.

[DBT Therapy](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/dbt-dialectical-behavior-therapy/)

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201200%20745'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

### Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT is a highly effective therapy that helps alleviate the symptoms of mental health disorders & address underlying causes of addiction.

[CBT Therapy](https://www.renaissancerecovery.com/drug/rehab/cbt-cognitive-behavioral-therapy/)
