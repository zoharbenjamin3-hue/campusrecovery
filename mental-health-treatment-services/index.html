Campus Recovery Center provides comprehensive adult mental health treatment through a full continuum of outpatient care in Lake Worth Beach, Florida. Our highly individualized approach is tailored to each client's unique needs, combining evidence-based clinical therapies with a supportive community environment.

Conditions We Treat

  • Depression (major depressive disorder, persistent depressive disorder)
  • Anxiety disorders (generalized anxiety, social anxiety, panic disorder)
  • Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
  • Bipolar disorders
  • Schizoaffective disorder
  • Schizophrenia
  • Co-occurring disorders (dual diagnosis: mental health combined with substance use)

Treatment Programs

PHP

Partial Hospitalization Program: 7 days/week, 5 hours/day of intensive mental health programming with structured individual and group therapy sessions.

IOP

Intensive Outpatient Program: 6 days/week, 3 hours/day with progressive step-down as mental health stabilizes and independence increases.

OP

Outpatient Program: 2 days/week of individual and group counseling for ongoing mental health maintenance and continued growth.

Our Mental Health Services

Individual Therapy

Private, one-on-one sessions with a licensed primary therapist assigned for the full duration of your program. Our therapists utilize evidence-based approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Motivational Interviewing, and trauma-focused therapies.

Group Therapy

Facilitated group sessions addressing core mental health topics including trauma processing, depression management, anxiety coping strategies, life skills development, grief work, and interpersonal effectiveness. Groups are held in a confidential, supportive setting with 12–15 clients.

Psychiatric Medication Management

Our psychiatric nurse practitioner evaluates each individual and collaborates to create medication protocols tailored to specific needs. Ongoing monitoring ensures optimal effectiveness and minimal side effects.

Case Management

Dedicated case managers help clients navigate practical challenges that can impede recovery, including assistance with identification documents, legal matters, benefits coordination, employment resources, and housing support.

Wellness Education

Psychoeducational programming that empowers clients with knowledge about their conditions, coping strategies, mindfulness practices, and healthy lifestyle habits that support long-term mental health stability.

Family Services

We recognize the vital role family plays in recovery. Our comprehensive family support services educate and support families throughout their loved one's treatment journey, helping to rebuild relationships and create a healthy home environment.

Our Clinical Team

Our therapists are licensed, master's-level educated, or Certified Addiction Professional (CAP) credentialed. The clinical team includes specialists in behavioral health, expressive arts therapy, movement-based psychotherapy, CBT, DBT, and trauma-informed care. Campus Recovery Center is recognized as one of the highest credentialed outpatient facilities in the region.

Our Approach to Mental Health Treatment

Mental health conditions are medical conditions — not character flaws, not weaknesses. At Campus Recovery Center, we treat mental health disorders with the same clinical rigor and evidence-based precision as any other medical condition. Our approach is built on several key principles:

  • Individualized treatment planning — No two clients present the same way. Your treatment plan is developed collaboratively between you, your primary therapist, and your psychiatric provider based on a comprehensive clinical assessment at intake.
  • Integrated care — Therapy and medication management work in tandem. Your therapist and psychiatric provider communicate regularly to ensure your treatment plan is cohesive and responsive to your progress.
  • Trauma-informed framework — Many mental health conditions have roots in trauma. Our entire clinical environment — from intake to discharge — is designed to be safe, predictable, and empowering.
  • Measurable outcomes — We track clinical progress using validated assessment tools and adjust treatment accordingly. Recovery isn't guesswork — it's guided by data.

Who Is This Program For?

Our mental health programs serve adults (18+) who:

  • Are experiencing symptoms of depression, anxiety, PTSD, bipolar disorder, or other conditions that interfere with daily functioning
  • Have been discharged from an inpatient psychiatric facility and need structured outpatient support
  • Are stepping down from a residential or PHP-level program at another facility
  • Have co-occurring substance use and mental health conditions requiring integrated treatment
  • Have not responded adequately to outpatient therapy alone and need a higher level of structure
  • Need psychiatric medication evaluation or adjustment in a monitored clinical setting

If you're unsure whether our program is the right fit, call (877) 995-0102 for a free, confidential assessment. Our admissions team can help determine the most appropriate level of care.

Mental Health and Substance Use: The Connection

Mental health conditions and substance use disorders frequently co-occur. Depression can lead to self-medication with alcohol. Anxiety can drive dependence on benzodiazepines. Trauma can fuel both PTSD and substance use simultaneously. At Campus Recovery Center, we never treat one condition in isolation. If a co-occurring substance use disorder is identified during your assessment, it will be integrated into your treatment plan from day one.
